Paris, France


Paris, often called the "City of Light," is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world. Known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture, Paris offers something for everyone.

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – The most famous avenue for shopping and leisure.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – A breathtaking view from Montmartre.
  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Escargot – A traditional French delicacy of snails.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory ingredients.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate cookies with creamy filling.
  • French Cheese – A wide variety of world-famous cheeses.
